Optimized Matching Algorithm

Our AI engine considers multiple factors to create optimal matches:

  • Technical skills and qualifications
  • Academic background and achievements
  • Location preferences and constraints
  • Sector interests and career aspirations
  • Equity and inclusion parameters

The system ensures fair distribution while maximizing satisfaction for both students and organizations.
